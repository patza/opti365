Just as choosing the right accessory can significantly influence the look of an outfit (in a good or bad way), so too can your attitude significantly impact your day. When you wake each morning, you can choose what type of attitude will accessorize your day. Is it always easy to reach for positivity ? No. It's often a difficult choice to make. But in stretching yourself, reaching for the harder choice, you'll transform the way you look-- and the way you see.
